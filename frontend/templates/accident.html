// General Response Guidelines

Immediate Safety

  • Stop vehicle: Turn off engine immediately.
  • Hazard lights: Switch on right away.
  • Move safe: Exit vehicle away from traffic.
  • Warning triangle: Place 45m behind if available.

Injured Persons

  • Don't move: Avoid moving anyone with spinal injury.
  • Bleeding: Firm pressure with clean cloth.
  • Breathing: Check and keep airway clear.
  • Unconscious: Recovery position if breathing.

Document the Scene

  • Photos: Capture damage, positions, road markings.
  • Exchange info: Names, plates, insurance details.
  • Witnesses: Names and contact numbers.
  • Report: File police and insurance report.
