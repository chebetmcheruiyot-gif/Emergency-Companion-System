// General Fire Safety

Using Extinguisher

  • PULL the safety pin.
  • AIM at the base of the fire.
  • SQUEEZE the handle slowly.
  • SWEEP side to side across the base.

Smoke Safety

  • Stay Low: Crawl under smoke for cleaner air.
  • Cover Nose: Use wet cloth if available.
  • Check Doors: Feel with back of hand first.
  • Seal Gaps: Block smoke with towels if trapped.

Escape Plan

  • Know Exits: Two ways out of every room.
  • Meeting Point: Agree a safe place outside.
  • Help Others: Children and elderly first.
  • Never Return: Do not go back inside.
